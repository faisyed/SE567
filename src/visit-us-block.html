Hours & Admission

Opening Hours

Open Daily 9:30–6:00

The Gallery will be closed Independence Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas, and New Year’s Day.

Admission Prices

Adults $20
Seniors (55+) $10
Members Free
Children (12 and under) $10
Veteran/First Responder $10

Visitors can park for free in the gallery parking lot.

Accessibility

  • We can lend you a wheelchair just ask at the information desk.
  • Visitors may borrow a standard manual wheelchair during their visit.
  • We have a prayer room. Let one of the team know if you’d like to use it.
  • Single occupancy / all gender restrooms are available.

Amenities &
Other Facilities

We provide a number of opportunities for you to interact with us in order to preserve and improve the visitor experience at the gallery because we want you to be a part of our gallery and feel its beauty.

For general questions and questions about accessibility, please reach out to the information desk.
